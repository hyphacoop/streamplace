---
title: Embedding your livestream
description: How to embed your livestream on your website, blog, etc.
---

Streamplace provides an easy way to embed your livestream on any website or
blog.

You can access the embedded livestream page by putting `/embed` in the URL of
your livestream. For example, if your livestream URL is
`https://stream.place/iame.li`, the embed URL will be
`https://stream.place/embed/iame.li`.

You can use the following HTML snippet to embed your livestream:

```html
<iframe
  src="https://stream.place/embed/your-handle"
  width="560"
  height="315"
  frameborder="0"
  allowfullscreen
></iframe>
```

Alternatively, you can use the share sheet located on your livestream page.
Click the "Share" button, and you'll find the embed code ready to copy.
